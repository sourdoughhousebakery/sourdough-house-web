Our Philosophy

We believe great bread takes time

Sourdough House started with a single jar of starter and a stubborn belief that the best things in life can't be rushed. Every loaf we bake goes through a long, slow fermentation — because that's where the flavor lives.

We don't take shortcuts. We don't use commercial yeast. We let our starter do what it does best, and we think you can taste the difference in every bite — from our crusty boules to our tangy discard cookies.

72-Hour Fermentation

Long, cold ferments for deeper flavor, better texture, and easier digestion.

Organic & Unbleached

We source the best flour we can find — always unbleached, always organic.

Small Batch Only

No mass production. Every item is made by hand, in limited quantities.

Zero Waste

Our discard becomes cookies, crackers, and more. Nothing goes to waste.
