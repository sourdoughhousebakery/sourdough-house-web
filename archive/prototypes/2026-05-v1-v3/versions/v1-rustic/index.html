Our Story

Born From a Starter, Raised With Love

What started as a pandemic hobby quickly became a passion — and then a calling. Every loaf from Sourdough House is made with our own carefully nurtured starter, slow-fermented for the best flavor and texture.

From artisan boules to sweet muffins and cookies, we believe that great baking takes time. No shortcuts, no compromises — just honest, handcrafted goodness that you can taste in every bite.

— The Sourdough House Family
